BATTLE TANK GAME DESIGN DOC
Udemy Unreal Course - Section 4

//Concept:
The player must drive their tank Actor around a space using realistic tank movement mechanics. The player's goal is to find and destroy the other enemy tanks before one of them destroys the player.

//Rules:
The player wins if all enemy tanks are destroyed and the player survives.
The player loses if any enemy tank destroys them.
The enemy tanks are confined to the same movement and aiming control restrictions as the player.
Finite health and ammo

//Requirements:
Tank asset
Tank Movement AI
Tank Attack AI
Firing and Damage Mechanics
Win and Lose States
Main Menu/Basic In Game GUI
Sound Effects
